Fishing on the loch

Spend a morning or a full day on the nearby loch. We can arrange a local ghillie, sort out permits and tackle, and pack a flask and lunch. Beginners are genuinely welcome — there's no better place to learn. Return to a hot meal and a dram by the fire.
