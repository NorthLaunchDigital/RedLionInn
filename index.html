Welcome

A proper Scottish retreat

Tucked into the quiet countryside of Angus, The Red Lion has long been a gathering place for walkers, anglers and anyone after a good meal by the fire. Stone walls, soft tartan and the smell of woodsmoke set the tone.

Whether you're here to fish the loch, play a round on the local courses, or simply slow down for a weekend, you'll find a genuine Highland welcome and a kitchen that takes the seasons seriously.
